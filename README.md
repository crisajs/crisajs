## About

I don't just write code — I build products that solve real problems for real people.

My work lives at the intersection of **software engineering, AI and social impact**: WhatsApp-native education platforms that train professional caregivers, revenue-intelligence tools for small businesses, and digital experiences for local companies in São Paulo. I care deeply about architecture, documentation and craft — software should be as elegant on the inside as it is on the outside.

Today I coordinate the **Instituto Saber & Cuidar** in São Paulo, Brazil, where I lead the institute's entire technology operation — proof that great engineering can transform lives, not just businesses.
